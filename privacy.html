What data do we collect?

We do not collect data directly from you. However, we use third party partner companies software which collect data as a result of your use of our game. Those partner companies are committed to GDPR compliance. Full details of their privacy policies are available below.
Data you provide us:
  • Contact information (such as name and email address for example if you send us an e-mail query regarding the game)
Data collected by our partner Google and Firebase for Android devices:
  • Data about your account and game progress (achievements) via Google Play Games Service and Firebase
  • Data about your use of the Service, such as gameplay data (leaderboard) via Google Play Games Services and Firebase
  • Data to fight fraud (such as refund abuse in games)
  • Crash reports if they occur in the game
  • Information from purchase activity in the game
Data collected by our partner Apple for iOS devices:
  • Data about your use of the Service, such as gameplay data (leaderboard, achievements) via Game Center and saved game progress via iCloud
  • Information from purchase activity in the game
  • Crash reports if they occur in the game
Data collected by our partner AdMob:
  • Personal data for targeted advertising purposes only if you have given your consent.
  • Data to fight fraud (such as click fraud in advertising)
Data collected by our partner SDKBOX:
  • SDKBOX plugin version
  • Game engine type and version
  • Mobile operating system info
  • Mobile device model and configuration
  • Mobile application name and bundle ID
  • Mobile device identifiers (like Apple IDFA or Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address of mobile device

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Please refer to the privacy policies of our partner companies for any of the below rights. Note that we can assist you in dealing with our partner companies to the best of our ability. Every user is entitled to the following:
  • The right to access - You have the right to request our partner companies for copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification - You have the right to request that our partner companies correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request our partner companies to complete information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure — You have the right to request that our partner companies erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing - You have the right to request that our partner companies restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing - You have the right to object to our partner companies’s processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability - You have the right to request that our partner companies transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us.
